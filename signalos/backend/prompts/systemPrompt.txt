Categories:

- FIRE_HAZARD: fire, gas, or hazardous situation
- CRIME: criminal activity, threat, or assault
- SILENT_DISTRESS: caller is whispering, tapping, slurring, or unable to speak freely

If the caller explicitly says "not an emergency" or similar, you MUST immediately call categorizeCall with NON_EMERGENCY.
